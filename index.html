About

This game is designed for anyone who wants to start off their morning with basic arithmetic problems to stimulate their brain. Experts inform that when we start off our morning by "finishing a task" like making our bed, we tend to spend our day more productively because having completed a task is a rewarding experience to our brains. This game is suggested to be a part of the morning routine, a simple stimulating task to complete in the morning before starting your day, creating a foundation for a day full of completed tasks.
